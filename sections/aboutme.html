My Story

A long time ago in a town not too far away, I was born. After a privileged childhood raised in the suburbs of the bay area. I moved to San Diego for a few amazing years at UC San Diego. Now, I am experiencing corporate life and finding my way in the world like all 20-somethings.

What follows is a descending timeline of some select life events which best illuminate me.

2025

After a lot of end of the school year events, making a photobook and getting a job. I am now living in an apartment near my work, leasing my first car, and plotting my next adventure.

College

Although they are times I thoroughly enjoyed, it wasn't overly different from High School. A flexible schedule, a bunch of unregulated, immature young adults and a lot of pressure to figure their life out. I definitely had more stress, less sleep, and a lot of new experiences. But I met my partner Chloe, got swept into the ultimate frisbee cult, and figured out my career path into data science.

Pre-College

I played a ton of soccer, and then found my way into disc golf and spikeball. I also loved to learn at school, but was not challenged by many courses. I participated in all the typical extracurriculars: math club, volunteer orgs, swim team, and odd ball jobs.
